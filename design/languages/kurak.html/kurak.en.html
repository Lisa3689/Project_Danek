Board game "Kurak"

The goal of the game is to attract interest and spread the ancient culture patchwork sewing.

A little history:
This name comes from the Kyrgyz word "kurak", which means "to connect (stitch) separate patches together." This technique of needlework has more than 2.5 thousand years and it is present in many cultures of ancient peoples.
